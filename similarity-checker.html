How Our Client-Side Algorithm Works

Instead of simply looking for exact keyword matches, our tool employs an N-Gram Shingling Sequence. This means we break down your text into 3-to-4 word phrases (shingles) and look for overlaps.

  • Detects "Spinning": If a writer just changes one word in a sentence (e.g., changing "happy" to "glad"), our algorithm still detects the surrounding structural similarity.
  • Visual Highlighting: The tool visually paints the stolen text red, allowing editors to know exactly which paragraph needs to be rewritten.
  • Privacy Guaranteed: By running the complex math calculations directly via your browser's JavaScript engine, your confidential business proposals, essays, and articles are never saved in any database.
