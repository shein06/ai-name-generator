Why Resize Your PDF?

Often, documents exported from different software or scanned via mobile apps end up with chaotic, non-standard page sizes. When you try to print or combine them with other documents, it causes printing errors or messy alignments. OmniTools AI provides a robust PDF Page Resizer to standardize your files into standard A4 or US Letter formats.

The "Vector Embedding" Advantage

Unlike standard tools that convert your PDF into a flat image, our tool utilizes a technique called Vector Page Embedding. This means we take your original page and embed it as a scalable vector object onto a fresh canvas of your desired size.

  • No Blurriness: Your text, charts, and logos remain infinitely scalable.
  • Text Searchability: The text layer is preserved, so you can still search (Ctrl+F) and copy text from the new PDF.
  • 100% Privacy: Using JavaScript (`pdf-lib`), the complex matrix calculations happen right inside your browser's memory. We do not upload your file to any remote server.
