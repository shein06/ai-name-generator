Secure Your Sensitive Information

Whether you are sharing financial reports, legal contracts, or personal records, securing your PDF documents is critical. Our Free PDF Password Protector adds a layer of encryption to your files, ensuring that only authorized people with the correct password can access the contents.

Advanced Permission Controls

OmniTools AI doesn't just stop at opening the file. With our advanced settings, you can define exactly what the viewer is allowed to do. You can prevent users from:

  • Printing: Stop unauthorized physical copies of your document.
  • Copying Text: Prevent people from highlighting and copying content to their clipboard.
  • Modifying: Lock the document layout to ensure your data cannot be altered or tampered with.

Privacy First Guarantee

We take your privacy seriously. Your files are processed entirely within your web browser. We do not upload, store, or have access to your PDF documents or the passwords you create. Everything remains on your device.
