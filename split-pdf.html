The Ultimate Visual PDF Splitter

Forget about guessing page numbers or writing confusing ranges like "1, 4-7, 10". Our Advanced Visual PDF Splitter provides a modern, intuitive workspace where you can actually see what you are doing. By integrating PDF.js and PDF-lib technologies, we offer an app-like experience directly in your browser.

Two Powerful Modes

  • Extract Mode: Perfect for pulling out specific chapters, invoices, or charts from a massive document. Only the pages you click will be saved into the new file.
  • Delete Mode: Ideal for removing blank pages, confidential information, or outdated slides. The pages you click will be removed, and everything else will be kept.

100% Privacy & Security

Despite its powerful visual features, this tool remains Client-Side only. Your files are never uploaded to any server. All the rendering, splitting, and deleting happens using your own device's computing power. This makes OmniTools AI the safest choice for processing sensitive legal, medical, and corporate documents.
