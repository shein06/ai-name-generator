Why "Core Web Vitals" are the New Rank Checker

In the past, website ranking checkers simply counted how many times a keyword appeared on your page. Today, Google's algorithm has evolved. Google introduced Core Web Vitals as an official, critical ranking signal. If your website is slow or has poor structural SEO, you will not rank on the first page, no matter how good your content is.

Understanding Your Scores

  • Green (90 - 100): Excellent. Google loves your page and will prioritize indexing and ranking it.
  • Yellow (50 - 89): Needs improvement. You have missing alt tags, unoptimized images, or render-blocking JavaScript.
  • Red (0 - 49): Poor. High risk of being penalized in search rankings. You need an immediate technical SEO audit.
