Why Client-Side Conversion is Better

Most online PDF converters require you to upload your sensitive contracts, resumes, or assignments to a remote server. This poses a massive security risk. Our tool is built differently:

  • 100% Offline Processing: Utilizing pdf.js and modern web technologies, the text extraction happens directly within your computer's RAM.
  • Instant Download: Because there is no uploading or downloading from a server, the conversion process is incredibly fast.
