Understanding Error Correction (ECL)

A unique feature of QR codes is their ability to sustain damage and still be scannable. If you are uploading a custom logo to put in the center of the code, you are technically "damaging" the center pixels. This is why we default our generator to Level H (High Error Correction), ensuring that even if 30% of the code is covered by your logo, mobile phone cameras can still scan it flawlessly.
