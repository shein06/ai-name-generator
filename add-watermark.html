The Most Secure PDF Watermark Creator

Protecting your intellectual property and confidential documents has never been easier. With OmniTools AI's Advanced PDF Watermark tool, you can securely stamp your files with custom text (like "CONFIDENTIAL" or "DRAFT") or your company logo.

Advanced Customization Features

  • Text & Image Support: Versatile stamping options. Support for PNGs with transparent backgrounds makes logos look professional.
  • Smart Layering: Adjust the opacity slider to create a translucent watermark that doesn't hide your document's core content.
  • Precision Positioning: Easily rotate the watermark diagonally across the page or anchor it cleanly to the corners.

Why Use Our Client-Side Architecture?

If you are stamping sensitive business contracts or unreleased manuscripts, uploading them to a random website is a huge security risk. Our tool leverages modern WebAssembly and JavaScript (`pdf-lib`) to process the PDF entirely inside your browser's memory. No data is transmitted, meaning zero risk of interception or unauthorized storage.
